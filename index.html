About NayePankh Foundation

We are a trusted NGO committed to uplifting children, women, and the differently-abled. With a focus on education, healthcare, and empowerment, we work to create a better world for those in need.

We offer education, skill development, and medical/legal aid to underprivileged communities, working towards a self-sustained ecosystem.
